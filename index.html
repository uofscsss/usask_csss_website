Welcome to the

CSSS

Hello fellow visitor,

My name is Lealand Adam. I am currently the 20th Computer Science Student Society President. The purpose of the Computer Science Student Society or the CSSS, is to encourage and strengthen community between students of computer science and other student, the Department of Computer Science at the University of Saskatchewan, and potential employers of the world. We strengthen our community base by hosting First Year Computer Science study sessions, social events and helping anyone in Math or Computer Science classes to the best of our knowledge.

Tweets by @UofSCSSS

Currently, our Society consist of 14 executive members, including myself, and over 100 CSSS members. If you are interested in becoming part of our society, stop by our office, S338 of 3rd Floor Spinks, and we will be more than willing to help you out. We welcome all students to come and join the CSSS. A membership costs only $5.00 and with that comes with many perks such as cheaper locker rentals, free admission to CSSS hosted events and access to past Computer Science exam files just to name a few. Our office provides an assortment of food and drinks for low prices. And if you're looking to take a break from studying in the Spinks' Computer Lab, just right outside our office is a Student Lounge which consists of a open sitting area, an air hockey table and a Ping Pong table. I hope this message enlightens you on our Society's view and our purpose. Have a good day!
